How to play 🡣

W and S | Main Thruster
A and D | Turning
Q and E | Strafing
O and P | Zooming
Space | Shoot
F | Secondary System
G | Control panel
C | Camera mode
Z | Percision mode
T | Use ship terminal
V | toggle main menu
l | Show/Hide aimlaser
~ | Debug mode
H | toggle this menu
Tip: Each system effects its own thing and will use your overall power usage.
Tip Jr: You are NOT immortal, pay attention to your health and oxygen meters!
--Low oxygen will cause your screen to darken slowly and will kill you if unremedied.
Press any key (or 'h' if you opened this menu) to this menu!
